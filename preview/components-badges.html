Why the padding rule matters
Symmetric pad Limited Trailing tracking pushes the glyph block left of optical centre.
Compensated Limited Right pad reduced by 0.1em (the tracking on the last glyph) — re-centres.
Rule. Any uppercase tracked-out pill takes its left/right padding asymmetrically: padding: 6px calc(12px − 0.1em) 6px 12px. Use line-height: 1 so vertical centring stays exact. The .badge.caps utility in this card handles both for you.
