The @theme block in tailwind/globals.css registers status colours as utilities — bg-success-subtle, text-danger-strong, border-info, etc. shadcn maps --destructive to danger; the other three status roles live alongside it. Drop globals.css into a shadcn project — no tailwind.config.js needed in v4. See tailwind/README.md.
